What makes a walkable city?

Connected Streets

Short blocks, frequent intersections and pedestrian shortcuts reduce walking distance and improve access.

Safety & Lighting

Calm traffic, clear crossings and good lighting increase perceived safety and real safety.

Local Amenities

Shops, transit and parks within a short walk replace car trips and enliven streets.

Green & Shade

Trees, seating and rain-friendly design make walking comfortable year-round.
